Convener at EME Olympiad

EME Olympiad is a 4 days extravaganza of a wide range of events extended from singing to robotics and much more, with an event catalog spanning over 60 events in catagories of sports, technical and arts events. Convener is appointed after elections, where all the students vote for their candidate. I was appointed Convener in my last year of bachelors studies and was responsible for arranging EME Olympiad in 2018. The main purpose of this event was to generate enough funds to sponsor the students who were unable to meet financial needs of their studies and to make sure they can complete their studies without worrying for their finances.
